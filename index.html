About Me

I am a full-stack web developer with a background in game development, graphic design, photography, user experience design, user interface design, and Japanese.

In the spring of 2021, I will graduate with a BS in Media Arts and Science, double specializing in web development and game development. After graduation, I will begin working towards an MS in Human-Computer Interaction. I have completed a minor in Japanese Studies.

My involvement and interest in international affairs and cross-cultural experiences have earned me a place as a Plater International Scholar. In the summer of 2017, I traveled to Miyoshi, Aichi, Japan, on behalf of the city government to strengthen relations between our sister cities. In the winter of 2020, I completed a semester at an intensive Japanese language school in Tokyo.

I hope for nothing more than to create applications and experiences to benefit others around the world.
