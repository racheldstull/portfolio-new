About

Nebula Tech is a fictional digital marketplace that sells "Nebula Tech" products. The company focuses on gaming technology, such as gaming laptops, powerful phones, and VR technology.

From the website, one is supposedly able to browse merchandise, review specifications of a product's design, and complete a purchase with customization options in checkout.
