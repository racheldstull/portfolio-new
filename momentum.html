About

"Momentum" is a website to assist individuals struggling with alcohol and/or drug addictions to begin and/or take steps along their road to recovery, as well as help concerned families and friends of people with addictions by providing them with resources and a community of individuals to reach out to.

In the future, a personal tracker for users to keep track of their sobriety status will be implemented, as well as postings for AA meetings and conferences, and information on how to recover.

The site currently features an online forum for members of the community to ask questions and post responses.
